After the conference

{{ title }}

DjangoCon US sprints are where the wider Django community comes together to build. Taking place after the main conference, the sprints are two dedicated days of collaboration on Django, Python, and community-driven open source projects for people of all experience levels.

Reserve your spot

Attending sprints is free all you need to do is secure your place by “purchasing” a ticket.

Two dedicated days

Build alongside the community.

Sprints are two days of hands-on collaboration on Django, Python, and the open source projects the community depends on. Pull up a chair, find a project that interests you, and start contributing.

Everyone is welcome, whatever your experience level. Whether it’s your first ever contribution or your hundredth, there’s a place for you and plenty of people happy to help you get started.
