We are excited to share an update regarding the format of this year's conference. To ensure we are making the most responsible use of our community resources, we have decided to move to a single-track format for DjangoCon US 2026. This decision was primarily driven by the need to optimize conference resources and manage significant A/V costs.

<!-- excerpt -->

Beyond the logistical benefits, a single-track conference creates a more cohesive and shared community experience. Attendees will no longer have to worry about missing a great talk or choosing between two simultaneous sessions; instead, we will all learn and grow together as one unified audience.

We can't wait to see you from Aug 24, 2026 to Aug 28, 2026 at the voco Chicago Downtown.
